Stet Sikkim

Sikkim has stood 3rd in the national ranking on elementary education for the year 2013-14 as per flash statistics released by Smt. Smriti Irani Hon'ble Union HRD Minister on 18.6.2014. The ranking is based on the four parameters of Accessibility, Infrastructure, Teacher and Outcome.
